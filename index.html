ABOUT ME

I'm a passionate filmmaker with a focus on visual storytelling that resonates emotionally and intellectually. With a background in both technical cinematography and creative direction, I approach each project as a unique opportunity to craft compelling narratives.

My journey in film began at Ball State University, where I honed my skills across all aspects of production. I believe in the power of collaboration and the magic that happens when talented individuals come together to create something greater than the sum of its parts.

When I'm not on set or in the editing suite, you'll find me exploring new camera techniques, studying classic cinema, or hiking in nature to recharge my creative energy.
